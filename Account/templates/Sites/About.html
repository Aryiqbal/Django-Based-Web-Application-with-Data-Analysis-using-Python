About Us

I am a university student currently enrolled at Westminster University, pursuing a degree in Computer Science. As part of my academic journey, I am working on an educational project focused on time series prediction within the foreign exchange (forex) market.

The forex market, also known as the foreign exchange or currency market, is a decentralized global marketplace where currencies are traded. It operates 24 hours a day, five days a week, and involves the buying and selling of currencies between participants, such as banks, financial institutions, corporations, governments, and individual traders.

The project aims to develop a time series prediction model using machine learning techniques to forecast future price movements in the forex market. By analyzing historical data and identifying patterns, the model seeks to provide insights into potential future trends, allowing traders and investors to make more informed decisions.

It's essential to note that while this project involves financial market analysis, it is strictly for educational purposes only. The information provided by the model should not be construed as financial advice or recommendations for trading decisions. Trading in the forex market carries inherent risks, and individuals should conduct thorough research and seek professional financial advice before engaging in any trading activities.
